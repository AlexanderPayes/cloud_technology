Minecraft

Minecraft is an open world sandblock game that is prodcedurally gentrated. It has 18,446,744,073,709,551,616 (2^64) seeds which are different worlds which players can play in. Players are set in a block like world to surive on their own or with friends. Multiplayer plays a huge role in this game.

Breif History of Minecraft

Minecraft was created in May 2009 by Markus Persson. Ever since then sales have skyrocketed and has been set down in history as one of the best seller games ever. Microsoft bought Minecraft in 2014 for 2.5 billion dollars.

Blocks

Players can build complex strcutures with any block they manage to get their hands on. There are always unqiue combations and almsot limitless amount of pallets to choose from.

Mobs/Monsters

There are over 79 mobs scattered across a Minecraft world. Some are more friendlier than the others annd some just want to kill you. There is a day and night cycle where passive mobs spawn during the day and hostile mobs spawn during the night.

Bosses

There are over 4 endgame bosses with each having their uniuqe rewards. Each has a different set of attacks and moves. The final boss the enderdragon is located in a different dimension where getting there itself is a troublesome task.
